Features

Everything you need to capture
your best thinking

Lumio combines smart linking, beautiful writing, and powerful organisation into one seamless workspace.

🔗

Auto-Linking

Lumio automatically detects connections between your notes and surfaces related ideas as you write — no manual tagging needed.

✍️

Distraction-Free Editor

A clean, minimal writing environment that gets out of your way. Markdown, slash commands, and rich embeds all built in.

🧠

AI Summarisation

Let AI distil long notes into crisp summaries, surface key insights, and suggest follow-up questions to deepen your thinking.

🗂️

Flexible Organisation

Use folders, tags, or graph view — choose what works for your brain. Switch between structures without losing any data.

🔒

Private by Default

End-to-end encryption on every note. Your thoughts belong to you — not advertisers. Zero-knowledge architecture throughout.

🌍

Works Everywhere

Available on Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android. Offline-first with instant sync when you reconnect — your notes are always with you.
